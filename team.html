Carol Ton
Senior Stylist · 30+ Years

With a few decades in the beauty industry, Carol's passion for hair is rooted in both artistry and science. She believes exceptional service starts with listening to your vision, your lifestyle, and how you want to feel when you leave the chair.

Read more

Carol continues to advance her education with industry-leading color and cutting techniques. Her goal is for every client to leave feeling confident, understood, and in love with their hair.

Carol is the sunshine in the salon - warm, friendly, and always bringing great energy to the team and clients. When she's not behind the chair, she enjoys spending time with her two sons and daughter, who live in California and Arizona. She is close with her mother and sister, both also in California, and loves painting and gardening in her free time.

Languages: English and Vietnamese fluently, and currently learning Spanish.

Rose Delfariban
Master Hairstylist · 30+ Years

With more than 30 years of experience in the beauty industry, Rose is a passionate and dedicated hairstylist who takes pride in helping every customer look and feel their best. She began her hairstyling career in Shiraz, Iran, in 1993, where she developed a strong foundation in hair design, coloring, and client care.

Read more

After moving to the United States with her family in 2012, Rose continued pursuing her passion and joined Hair Xpressions Salon, where she has been serving customers ever since. Her commitment to excellence, attention to detail, and personalized approach have earned her a loyal clientele and a reputation for exceptional service.

Rose specializes in hair coloring, highlights, precision haircuts, styling, and elegant updos. Rose is known for her warm personality and welcoming atmosphere and ensures her customers leave the salon feeling confident and beautiful.

Outside the salon, Rose loves spending time with her family and friends. She loves to get some sun and bird-watch in her backyard. Languages: English and Farsi.

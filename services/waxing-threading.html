The Sabina Method

Organic threading the salon is known for.

Threading is older than waxing, gentler on sensitive skin, and delivers a precision shape that waxing can't match. Owner Sabina Thapaliya specializes in organic threading - using only cotton thread, no chemicals, no heat.

It's particularly suited for sensitive skin, hormonal skin, anyone using retinols or actives, and clients who simply want the cleanest, most precise brow line possible. The technique is also fast - most brow shapes take under fifteen minutes.

We serve clients from Fairfax, Burke, Springfield, Annandale, and the wider Braddock Road corridor. Brow threading is one of our most-booked services.

Which Is Right For You?

Threading or waxing - how to choose.

Choose Threading If…

  • ✓ You have sensitive or reactive skin
  • ✓ You're using retinols, tretinoin, or actives
  • ✓ You want the cleanest, most precise brow line
  • ✓ You're prone to ingrowns from waxing
  • ✓ You want a chemical-free, organic option

Choose Waxing If…

  • ✓ You're removing hair from larger areas (legs, arms, back)
  • ✓ You want longer-lasting results between appointments
  • ✓ You prefer the speed of bulk hair removal
  • ✓ You don't have skin sensitivities to wax
  • ✓ You're getting a full-body service before vacation

Not sure? We'll guide you during your consultation - and you can always start with one and try the other.

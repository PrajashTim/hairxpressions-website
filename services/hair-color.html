Our Approach

Consultation-first color with real staying power.

Every hair appointment starts with a conversation about what you want, what your hair can realistically hold, and how much maintenance you want between visits. That is how we keep color looking dimensional, flattering, and easy to live with after you leave the salon.

Square currently shows haircut, blowdry, touch-up, demi color, whole color, highlights, toning, and correction services. Carol Ton, Rose Delfariban, and Norra are part of the current bookable hair and color lineup, with Carol bringing Fairfax salon experience dating back to 1985.

We use trusted professional color lines and tailor the formula, timing, and finish to the condition of your hair rather than forcing every client into the same recipe.

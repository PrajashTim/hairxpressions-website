Our Approach

Forty years of teaching color in Fairfax.

Every appointment starts with a consultation. We listen to what you actually want - and we tell you honestly what your hair can hold. That's the only way to deliver color that looks natural, grows out without harsh lines, and doesn't damage your hair.

Lisa Nguyen, our lead color specialist, is a certified color educator - meaning she teaches other stylists how to do color. Carol Ton has been doing color in Fairfax since 1985. The depth of experience in our chairs is the reason clients come from across Fairfax, Burke, Springfield, and Annandale.

We use Goldwell, Wella, and L'Oréal Professional - the same color lines used in the best salons in the country.

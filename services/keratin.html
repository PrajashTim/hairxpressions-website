The Treatment Difference

What you get back: time.

A well-done smoothing or restorative treatment can cut daily styling time dramatically. It can also help tame frizz, support a glossier finish, and make the hair feel more cooperative between appointments.

The important part is matching the treatment to your hair history. Timing, application, and processing all change depending on whether the hair is color-treated, porous, thick, fine, relaxed, or already heat stressed.

Keratin results often last 3 to 5 months depending on the service selected, your aftercare, and the texture you start with.

What To Expect

The keratin appointment, step by step.

A keratin appointment usually starts with a clarifying wash, then a section-by-section application tailored to your hair density and texture. The finish is sealed with controlled heat so the smoothing result lasts.

Most clients notice easier styling, reduced frizz, and a smoother finish almost immediately. Aftercare matters, so we will explain exactly how to protect the result once you are home.

A consultation or strand test is a smart first step if your hair has been heavily colored or chemically treated before.

What To Expect

The keratin appointment, step by step.

A keratin smoothing treatment is a 2.5 to 4 hour appointment. We start with a thorough wash and clarifying shampoo to fully open the hair cuticle. Next, a protein-rich keratin solution is brushed through every section of your hair, then sealed in with low, controlled heat from a flat iron.

Most clients notice a dramatic reduction in frizz, faster styling time, and softer hair the very next day. For the first 72 hours, avoid washing or tying your hair up so the treatment can fully set.

A consultation strand test is always recommended for first-time clients, especially if your hair has been previously chemically treated.

The Bridal Promise

Forty years of getting it right the first time.

The most important thing we do for brides is the trial. Two hours of testing the look - hair, makeup, accessories - until you see exactly what you'll see in the mirror on your wedding day. No surprises. No "let's see how it works out." Just confidence.

Our bridal stylists have prepared brides in Northern Virginia for intimate ceremonies and large traditional weddings alike. We work in any style, any vision, and across cultural traditions.

On the day of: we arrive on time, set up quickly, and stay until everyone is finished. The whole bridal party leaves feeling pampered.

Your Bridal Timeline

What to do, when.

A simple timeline we'll walk you through once you book.

6+ Months Out
Initial consultation. Discuss vision, venue, dress style, hair length goals. Lock in your wedding date with us.
3 Months Out
Hair trial. We test the style with your dress neckline in mind. Consider keratin or color refresh if needed.
6 Weeks Out
Brow lamination (optional). Final color refresh. If doing lashes, book a "preview set" so you know how to care for them.
1 Week Out
Brow shape, facial, and bridal makeup trial if not yet done. Confirm timing & logistics for day-of.
Wedding Day
We arrive on time, set up quickly, and stay until you and your party walk out the door looking exactly right.

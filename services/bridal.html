Your Bridal Timeline

What to do, when.

A simple structure we can walk through once the consultation is booked.

6+ Months Out
Initial consultation. Discuss your vision, venue, timing, and whether any prep services should happen ahead of the wedding.
3 Months Out
Hair and makeup trial. Test the look, refine the shape, and adjust accessories or prep services as needed.
6 Weeks Out
Final color refresh planning, optional brow or lash prep, and confirmation of how many people need services.
1 Week Out
Confirm timing, logistics, and the final sequence for the event day so nothing feels rushed.
Wedding Day
The team focuses on timing, polish, and keeping the atmosphere calm while everyone gets finished.

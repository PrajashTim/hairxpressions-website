Eyebrow lamination, in plain English.

Eyebrow lamination is essentially a perm for your brows. We brush the hairs straight up into the shape you want them to grow, then apply a gentle solution that softens the bond inside each hair so it holds the new shape. The whole process is 30-45 minutes.

Once laminated, your brows sit fluffy and full for 6-8 weeks - perfect for anyone with sparse, downward-growing, or unruly brows. Pair it with tint or henna for a complete "I just woke up like this" look that doesn't require any morning makeup.

A patch test is recommended for first-time clients with sensitive skin.

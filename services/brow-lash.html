Eyebrow lamination, in plain English.

Eyebrow lamination is essentially a perm for your brows. We brush the hairs into the shape you want, then use a gentle solution that helps them hold that direction for weeks.

It is a strong option for sparse, downward-growing, or unruly brows. Pair it with tint or henna for a fuller finish that still looks soft and believable in daylight.

A patch test is recommended for first-time clients with sensitive skin.
